REST endpoints

Use these endpoints to integrate with external tools or to fetch upload metadata.

  • GET /api/calls/ — list uploaded calls
  • POST /api/calls/ — upload a file via multipart form field audio_file
  • GET /api/calls/<uuid>/ — get details for a specific call
